About us

We are Luka and Ravi. We have known each other since the 4th grade of high school and have become friends ever since. We both have a great passion for sports. We are both into kickboxing and also go to the gym often. Ravi trains 5 times a week and Luka 6 times a week. In addition to exercising, we also try to pay attention to our diet and work on discipline, so that we can achieve one goal: a healthy, strong body. Our PWS, a business plan for the company 'BUILD', is aimed at this. We chose this subject because we can both work hard and have roughly the same ambitions. That's why we thought this suited us best and it is a topic on which we can work well together. We have created a theoretical business plan for sports nutrition. Particularly for strength athletes, endurance athletes and athletes focused on stretching and flexibility. We have investigated the best ingredients and recipes for sports meals for these types of athletes to continue to perform optimally. After writing our PWS about this subject we decided to actually launch this business and make a webshop.
