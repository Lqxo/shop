About us

i am Luka. i have a great passion for sports. i am into kickboxing and also go to the gym often. i train 6 times a week. In addition to exercising, i also try to pay attention to my diet and work on discipline, so that i can achieve one goal: a healthy, strong body. my PWS, a business plan for the company 'BUILD', is aimed at this. i chose this subject because i can work hard and i like training. That's why i thought this suited me the best and it is a topic on which i can work well on. i have created a theoretical business plan for sports nutrition. Particularly for strength athletes, endurance athletes and athletes focused on stretching and flexibility. i have investigated the best ingredients and recipes for sports meals for these types of athletes to continue to perform optimally. After writing our PWS about this subject i decided to actually launch this business and make a webshop.
